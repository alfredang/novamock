Tax Scheme

Partial Tax Exemption (PTE) applies to all companies.
75% exemption on first $10,000 of chargeable income.
50% exemption on next $190,000 of chargeable income.
Maximum exemption: $102,500.
Start-Up Tax Exemption (SUTE) — for qualifying new companies in their first 3 consecutive YAs.
75% exemption on first $100,000 of chargeable income.
50% exemption on next $100,000 of chargeable income.
Maximum exemption: $125,000.

Conditions: company incorporated in Singapore, has ≤20 shareholders (all individuals or at least one holding ≥10%).
